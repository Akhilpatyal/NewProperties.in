About us

Total Environment’s Down by the water is a new ultra-luxury apartment project situated by Jakkur Lake, just off Airport Road in North Bangalore.

Set on an expansive 10-acre property in Jakkur, Down by the water embodies the pinnacle of luxury living by Total Environment Projects, offering spacious 3 BHK apartments with high-end features. This sustainability-focused development blends beautiful landscapes, abundant open spaces, and stunning lake views, creating an exclusive, serene environment.

The project’s location offers outstanding connectivity, positioned in the heart of Jakkur beside Jakkur Lake. It sits at the intersection of key areas such as Thanisandra, Kogilu, Bellary Road, Yelahanka, and Airport Road, making it conveniently close to existing and upcoming communities and amenities.
